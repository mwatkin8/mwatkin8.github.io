Translational Informatics

We are working to introduce a new wave of data types into the health ecosystem. At the forefront is the human genome and social determinants of health. Robust clinical genomics offers a veritable blueprint of the biological makeup of a patient to guide precision medicine efforts. An equally promising field, social determinants of health, presents the opportunity to capture the "metadata" of an individual's lifestyle (income, social relationships, living situation, etc.). This lifestyle data, when properly formalized and standardized, can be as informative as any genomic variant.

Clinical Interoperability

While the transition from paper to electronic medical records is certainly an impressive accomplishment of the past decade, the medical informatics community now faces an even more daunting task—interoperability. Data architects, terminologists, and informaticists have come together in an unprecedented effort to develop clinical data models and RESTful exchange protocols that emphasize extensibile standardization. With these standards now entering federal legislation, previously siloed innovations are turning collaborative and clinical interoperability is approaching reality.

Semantic Engineering

Knowledge resources play an increasingly critical role as more and more previously disparate electronic systems must now communicate and exchange information. Anyone who has been required to map from data source to data target can attest that manual approaches are not sustainable. To fully utilize the promise of interoperability and true machine learning, our biomedical knowledge sources need a semantic overhaul. Concepts that have enabled our modern way of life—linked data, semantic reasoning—must mature in the health domain.

App Gallery

Select from the gallery below to explore limited-functionality demos

LocalVar

A local variant collection manger to asynchronously detect synonyms, HGVS expression changes, and variant interpretation changes from ClinVar.

View on Github

Determinantes Sociales

A FHIR-based app for collecting social determinantes of health via FHIR Questionnaire and displaying a categorical summary. Designed, built, and deployed for a partner clinic in Lima, Peru.

View on Github

FHIR Lab Reports

FHIR Lab Reports is a standards-based interoperable application that transforms medical laboratory test results into computable data points, transmits them to the ordering clinical system via SMART on FHIR, and makes them accessible to clinicians via CDS Hooks.

View on Github

ResultsMyWay

ResultsMyWay is a newborn screening (NBS) application that uses CQL to automate the search for maternal and infant health factors that may influence the interpretation of NBS results. It also translates those results to FHIR resources and acts as a hub for several types of informational resources about the rare disorder that a given infant may be diagnosed with.

View on Github

VRS Tool Suite

Three web tools that implement the GA4GH Variant Representation Specification (VRS). The first adds VRS identifiers to a VCF file, the second converts an HGVS expression to VRS identifiers, and the third allows input fields to create VRS identifiers for a custom variant.

View on Github

Interactive GFF3 Viewer

Facilitates the manual exploration of a gff3 file. Shows file-wide statistics, a search/filtering view for individual features, and a tree view to show parent/child relationships.

View on Github
